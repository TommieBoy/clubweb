Dwight_DXCC_Award.jpeg Congratulations to Dwight Morrow, VE7BV , on receiving his DX Century Club Award under his TG9BBV callsign!

He tells us that there was some kind of "glitch" in his TG9BBV file with the ARRL and it delayed his receipt of the award.

He has 119 countries worked, and 112 countried confirmed.

Way to go Dwight!
