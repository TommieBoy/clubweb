Story 819

Winter Field Day - January 24/25

Wed, 2026-01-21 - by VA7AV

Winter Field Day was held from 8 AM Saturday to 2 PM Sunday with operations from Peter's VE7DNZ farm south of town.

The club monitored 146.52 simplex and the Mt. Lolo repeater 147.32 (+600). Members and visitors were invited to participate or observe over the weekend.

Story 815

Repeater Update

Wed, 2025-10-01 - by VA7AV

The Repeater Committee relocated VE7TSI to Mt. Dufferin and reconfigured VE7RKA hardware on Greenstone Mountain.

Analog FM operation now requires a 100 Hz PL tone on updated systems, with C4FM digital also supported.
