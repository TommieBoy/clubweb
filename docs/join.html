Become a Member

Interested in becoming a member of the Kamloops Amateur Radio Club? You do not need to be a licensed amateur radio operator to join. Electronics enthusiasts, shortwave listeners, and future hams are all welcome.

Membership Fees

Membership fees (effective September 2023) are $50 per year, renewed annually on September 1.

KARC members who are also current RAC members receive a $10 discount.

Family members residing at the same address can join at a discounted rate of $20 each when purchased with a main membership.

Member Benefits

  • Access to club repeaters
  • Access to private autopatch, IRLP, and AllStar linking
  • Access to the club kiwiSDR receiver
  • Access to the remote HF station for members with Advanced Qualification
  • Mentoring support for new hams

How to Join

Download and complete the membership form, then email it to memberships@karc.ca.

Membership fees can be sent by e-transfer to payments@karc.ca.
